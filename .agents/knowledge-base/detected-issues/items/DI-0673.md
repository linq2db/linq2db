## Why it matters
The `SqlConcat` enum value is intentionally placed at the end of `QueryElementType` rather than next to its peers (`SqlCast`, `SqlCoalesce`) to preserve LinqService integer ordinal wire-compat in v6.x. The comment defers the cleanup to v7. If v7 lands without addressing this, the enum ordering stays inconsistent and future additions may repeat the same workaround pattern.

## Suggested fix
In v7, move `SqlConcat` to the logical position adjacent to `SqlCast` and `SqlCoalesce`. Verify LinqService serialization round-trips are covered by tests before reordering, since ordinals are serialized as `int`.
